For some general guidelines on what things mean.

Keywords
  • General: This store is probably a single-brand store with a range of clothing that can cover a few similar styles.
  • Everything: This store stocks multiple brands with many different styles. Includes general.
  • Streetwear: An umbrella keyword used for when a store does not have a distinct and describable style, but is a more casual style.
Price Guidelines

Pricing category is hard to get perfect, but I try to adhere to these guidelines.

  • $ = Pretty much the lowest priced shops. Those who are new to fashion and don't want to spend much should check these out. Shirts will usually be $50 or below and outerwear will usually not go above $150 dollars or so.
  • $$ = Not comfortable dropping hundreds of dollars per item, but want to go a step above Uniqlo and Gap. Shirts will be nearing or around $100 mark and outerwear will generally be in the $100-$250 range.
  • $$$ = Designers, big-name brands, and more. Shirts will be at least $150 and above and outerwear will cost $300+. $$$ covers lots and lots of brands and designers.
  • $$$$ = Reserved for the most significantly expensive brands. Shirts will usually be $300+ and outerwear will be over $1000
Notes
  • I know the UK is not a country.
  • I know the descriptions and price are a bit inconsistent. It's hard to be both concise and accurate.
  • The list is a work-in-progress. It will be updated over time.
  • "Country" Does not necessarily mean that the store only ships to that country.
  • If you spot a mistake or have a suggestion, message me on reddit.
